Squirrel Earth

A heartwarming tale of rodents, acorns, and human annihilation.

The quick brown squirrel jumped over the fence and attacked the lazy human. He was furious and furry.

The lazy human fought valiantly to defend himself from the squirrel, but the Mr. Mime stood no chance against the enemy Pachirisu's Volt Tackle.

The lazy human fainted.

THE END.
